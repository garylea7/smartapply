Job Title: Senior Product Manager - SaaS Platform

Company: TechInnovate Solutions
Location: Remote (US/EU) with quarterly on-site meetings
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary Range: $120,000 - $160,000 annually plus equity and benefits

About Us:
TechInnovate Solutions is a rapidly growing SaaS company specializing in AI-powered business intelligence tools. Our flagship product helps enterprises transform raw data into actionable insights through intuitive dashboards and automated reporting. With over 500 enterprise clients and a 97% retention rate, we're expanding our product team to accelerate our roadmap and maintain our competitive edge.

Role Overview:
We're seeking an experienced Senior Product Manager to own our core analytics platform. You'll work closely with engineering, design, data science, and customer success teams to define product strategy, prioritize features, and deliver exceptional user experiences that solve real business problems. This role reports directly to the VP of Product and will have significant impact on company direction and growth.

Key Responsibilities:
• Own the product roadmap for our analytics platform, balancing customer needs, technical feasibility, and business objectives
• Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and customer interviews to identify opportunities and validate product decisions
• Develop detailed product requirements and user stories in collaboration with engineering and design
• Define success metrics for new features and analyze post-launch performance
• Manage the product development lifecycle from ideation to launch and iteration
• Present product plans and progress to executive leadership and key stakeholders
• Collaborate with marketing on go-to-market strategies and product messaging
• Work with customer success to gather feedback and ensure successful product adoption
• Stay current on industry trends, emerging technologies, and competitive landscape

Required Qualifications:
• 5+ years of product management experience, with at least 3 years in B2B SaaS products
• Proven track record of shipping successful products that drive business growth
• Strong analytical skills with experience using data to inform product decisions
• Excellent communication and presentation skills, with ability to articulate complex concepts clearly
• Experience with agile development methodologies and product management tools (Jira, Asana, etc.)
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Business, or related field (or equivalent experience)
• Demonstrated ability to collaborate effectively across functions and influence without authority

Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience with analytics, business intelligence, or data visualization products
• MBA or advanced degree in relevant field
• Background in data science, analytics, or software engineering
• Experience with AI/ML product development
• Knowledge of enterprise sales cycles and customer success practices
• Previous startup experience or leadership role in fast-growing company
